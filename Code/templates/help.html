Help Page

How to use the nav bar?

  • The nav bar is located at the top left corner of the home page
  • click on "Generate a random memory" will bring you to a page where you can see a random memory from other people
  • Click on "Filter by category" will bring you to a page where you can see a memory in a specific category
  • Click on "Contribute a memory" will bring you to a page where you can share your own happy memory
  • The "home" button in the lower left of the page will take you back to the home page

How to report a problem?

If you find something wrong with the functionalities of the web page, or you have something you want to share with us, you can reach out through email, which is listed on the lower left corner of the reporting. Email: wud3@carleton.edu
